BP Centiles App

Painlessly view pediatric blood pressure readings in context, normalized according to NIH guidelines to take a child's age, gender, and height into account. Instead of raw mmHg values, you'll see each measurement reported as a percentile for rapid classification of hypertension and prehypertension.

Cardiac Risk App

Instantly calculate and visualize a Reynolds Risk Score with SMART Cardiac Risk. Incorporate patient demographics from the medical record, and run what-if scenarios to illustrate the benefits of smoking cessation and blood pressure control.

Twinlist Med Rec App

Simplify the process of medication reconciliation by visualizing the overlap between two medication lists. Twinlist provides automated detection of brand-name and generic equivalents, and highlights similar drugs to help you spot any duplicated or unintended meds.
